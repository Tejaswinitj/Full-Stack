Our Story

Our heritage. Our story begins in 1971 along the Cobblestone streets of Seattle's historic Pike Place market. It was here where Starbucks opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. It was founde by Business partners Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl and Gordon Bowker who first met as students at the University of San Fransico. The trio were inspired to sell high-quality coffee beans and eqiupment by coffee roasting entrepreneur Alfred Peet.
